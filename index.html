Who are we


Who are we
The Event Architect is a Ugandan based company that started its genesis in the fall of 2015. Fueled by enthusiastic youth it aims to create the best and most sought after events agency in the industry.

Today it is considered one of the best event management agencies as its well trained and qualified staff strive to deliver quality results that has seen its growth surge in a short while.

The Event Architect is our personal mechanic for that event and that's why we say we are always "yours truly"
