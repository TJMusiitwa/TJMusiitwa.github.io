About

Who are we


Who are we
The Event Architect is a Ugandan based company that started its genesis in the fall of 2015. Fueled by enthusiastic youth it aims to create the best and most sought after events agency in the industry.

  • Our Mission

    Create a hustle free event planning environment, keeping in tune with the market needs, utilizing latest technology and trends whilst ensuring the client receives desirable attention.

  • Our Objectives

    • Be one of the top three event management specialists in Uganda.
    • Produce the same quality results every time
    • Justly compensate the employees, owners, founders and frequent clients of The Event Architect.

  • Our Principles

    • Consistency
    • Creativity
    • Courage
    • Focus
    • Innovation
    • Integrity
    • Leadership
    • Logistics
    • Respect
    • Trustworthiness
